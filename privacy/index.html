Home Keep Maintenance Tracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not ask for your name, email address, or any personal details to use this app.

All information you enter into this app — including your property address, appliance records, task history, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device. Your data never leaves your phone. We have no servers, no cloud storage, and no access to your information.
